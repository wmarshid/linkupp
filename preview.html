Sign up

Right off the bat we show you who there is to meet where you currently are. Once you've found potential contacts, registration is simple. Just make sure you have something to say!

Registration

All we need is your name and contact details, authentication is handled for you. Start filling out your profile with skills, interests or hobbies or put our app to work for you and let it get these from your existing online profiles.

Matches

The fun part! Our super smart algorithm has already found you some people to talk to, excellent. Now indicate which of them you would actually like to meet, sit back and relax. You will be notified once a mutual match is found. Never miss another great connection.

Profile

You have complete customisation over your own profile with the power to hide or display any attributes you wish i.e. seperate your business and personal lives. Your location is revealed only when you choose so or by checking into one of the popular social apps.

Alert Box

This displays notifications from other users who would like to linkupp where you are, whether to meet them or not is up to you. The linkupp system works similar to friending people on facebook and is completely asynchronous. The app automatically detects mutual matches and the connection is made only when both people wish to meet one another.
