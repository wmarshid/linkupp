Waqas Arshid

Waqas is entering his final year at the University of St Andrews majoring in Computer Science. He has spent this summer working as a software architect with the American International Group (AIG) based in their eBusiness department in New York City.

An avid participant in hackathons, he has been a finalist of the Samsung bada challenge and the SVC2UK appathon, attending an awards ceremony held at 10 Downing Street by Prime Minister David Cameron, came third at UCL FIndS' Hacking the City and most recently was awarded 'Best use of Technology' by Nokia at Battlehack NYC.
