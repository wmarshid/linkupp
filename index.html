LinkuppMeet anyone, anywhere, anytime.

Linkupp brings socialising into the 21st century. Through Linkupp you can meet and get talking to like minded people anywhere anytime. Linkupp uses your check-ins on popular apps such as foursquare to show you the people in the same location that you may be interested in meeting.

The app notifies you should a user in the same location want to meet you. Our powerful algorithm crunches through all your preferences and only identifies strong matches for you. In many locations Linkupp incentivises meeting a match through the use of location-based offers.

Put the Social back into Social Networking by conversing face to face with other app users. Linkupp imitates and accelerates the real world to introduce you to the people you should know in a friendly, safe and casual environment.
